When you select Show Group By Time and re-run the report all reported events are grouped by time, as shown in Figure 2.6-6. Since we know that all four events in the report occurred on the same day, this chart shows only one bar, labeled with that date. In a live environment, this format is particularly useful in identifying time-related enforcement spikes, which are similar to the user-related red flags. Spikes in policy enforcement at specific times such as evenings or weekends (especially with regard to Deny policies) can indicate inten­tional efforts at unauthorized access to sensitive resources.
