QuickStart topics

The QuickStart topics explain how to use sample ABAC policies and configure an application to make authorization requests in NextLabs CloudAz.

To access the QuickStart topics:

  1. Log in to the CloudAz console.

  2. If the Getting Started page is not displayed, click the Help link on the top right, then click Getting Started.

  3. On the right side of the page, click the QuickStart link.
